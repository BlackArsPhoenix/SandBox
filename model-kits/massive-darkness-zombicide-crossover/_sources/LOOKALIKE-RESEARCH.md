# Lookalike research (BGG / Cults / Steam / fan sites)

Searched 2026-07-12 for STLs that **visually match original CMON sculpts** from Green Horde / Wulfsburg / Friends and Foes (not generic orc/wolf proxies).

## Verdict

**No free public STL pack was found that recreates the retail Green Horde / Wulfsburg / Friends and Foes zombie sculpts at a recognizable 1:1 likeness.**

Evidence from fan communities:

1. **Tabletop Simulator Green Horde mod** (Steam Workshop) — author explicitly: *“All models for Green Horde are Custom. If anyone eventually 3D scans the real models I'll add them.”* A later thread (“Potential 3d scans”) discussed private Form/photogrammetry scans of Kickstarter minis, but **no public STL dump was published**.
2. **BGG** — files sections for Green Horde / Wulfsburg / Friends and Foes contain rules, organizers, tokens, dashboards — **not miniature recreations**.
3. **Tesera / RU community** — same: reviews and photos of plastic, no downloadable lookalike sculpt packs for Orc Walkers / Wolfz / Tainted.
4. **Printables / Thingiverse free** — organizers, terrain, tokens; free “zombie orc/wolf” minis are **generic DnD proxies**, not CMON poses/sculpts (this is what currently sits in `_shared-stls/` as temporary stand-ins).

## Closest fan recreations that *do* claim original likeness (paid)

These are the only creators found who advertise remakes **based on original Zombicide sculpts**. They do **not** cover the full retail gap list, but are the right direction:

| Creator | Platform | What exists | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **ciccioneroladro** | [Cults](https://cults3d.com/en/users/ciccioneroladro/3d-models) | Green Horde KS exclusives: Abominarat, Chromatiz, Magenta Covfefe of Aaargh (“raw… based on original”) | Paid (~€1.25–1.43). **Not** retail Orc Walker / Goblin Runner / Fatty / Necromancer / Tainted / Wolfz. |
| **MiChan** | [Cults](https://cults3d.com/en/users/MiChan/3d-models) | Many “inspired by Zombicide” packs: Berserker Walker/Runner/Fatty/Abomination, Promo Walkers/Runners/Fatty/Abomination, DogZ/CrowZ, survivors | Paid. Style matches **modern / Black Plague / Berserker** line better than Green Horde orcs. Author offers commissions: *“If you want a concrete model write to me”*. |
| CGTrader “Promo Zombie *” sets | CGTrader | Promo Walkers / Runners / Fatty / Abomination sets named for Zombicide | Paid, large files; appear aimed at **modern promo** sculpts, not Green Horde orcs/wolves. |

## Gap list still without a public lookalike STL

- Wulfsburg: **Wolfz**, **Wolfbomination**
- Green Horde retail: **Orc Walkers**, **Orc Fatty**, **Goblin Runners**, **Orc Abomination**, **Orc Necromancer**
- Friends and Foes: **Tainted Walkers**, **Tainted Abomination**
