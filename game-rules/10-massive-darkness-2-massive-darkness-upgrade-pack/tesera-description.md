# Описание с Tesera

Добавлено в коллекцию: 2026-02-10T21:19:03

URL: https://tesera.ru/game/2061223

Massive Darkness Upgrade Pack provides 369 cards for all the miniatures (Heroes and Enemies) from Massive Darkness (and its expansions) to be usable with Massive Darkness 2: Hellscape.


It also includes 3 Boss Dashboards converting 4 enemies from the original Massive Darkness to Boss encounters (in addition to their Roaming Monster versions). These are The Abyssal Demon, The Hellephant and a deadly duo of the Spearmaiden Cyclops and the Cliffbreaker Cyclops fighting as one Boss.


Finally it includes a Prologue Campaign Booklet (converting the Quests from the original Massive Darkness and A Quest of Crystal & Lava and 30 Campaign cards.


 


Lightbringers get another chance to fight the old enemies.
